What do common shareholders search on Google when they are afraid of the future?

When people are interested in a given stock, they will google its ticker and related news but also search about the market in general. We can actually know if the market is bullish or bear by looking at this searches. In the case of the Dow Jones index which measures the stock performance of 30 large companies listed on stock exchanges in the United States, the context is more important.
